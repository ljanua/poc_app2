# App Name Brainstorm

## Summary
The app should feel trustworthy, modern, and focused on player development and coaching insight. The name should suggest growth, performance, and precision while sounding premium and technically polished.

## Naming Direction
A strong direction is to use a name that combines:
- measurable progress
- coaching intelligence
- a sleek, modern product feel

## Name Options
1. **VantageIQ**
   - Feels premium and data-driven
   - Suggests insight and competitive advantage
   - Strong for a high-end sports performance product

2. **AthenaFlow**
   - Sounds intelligent and modern
   - Suggests structured movement and performance tracking
   - Slightly more brand-forward and polished

3. **PeakLogic**
   - Combines performance with analytical depth
   - Feels tech-forward and professional
   - Slightly more technical than human-centered

4. **CoachGrid**
   - Suggests a systemized view of player progress
   - Feels modern and operational
   - Works well for a coaching dashboard experience

5. **NexTrack**
   - Short, premium, and futuristic
   - Implies continuous performance monitoring
   - Good for a product-led brand

## Recommendation
**VantageIQ** is the strongest option because it feels premium, intelligent, and clearly aligned with performance insight.

## Notes
If you want something more athletic and crisp, **NexTrack** is a strong backup. If you want a more elegant and brand-led feel, **AthenaFlow** is a strong alternative.
